NHI-Reptilians, Dracos and Amphibians

Their Program on Earth

By Hybrids Rising

 



There is an extraordinarily rich history regarding Reptilian appearing alien beings dating back to the oldest known creation stories involving the Anunnaki. The Anunnaki were ancient deities of Sumerian, Assyrian, Akkadian and Babylonian culture. You can read a very basic overview of the Anunnaki at Wikipedia’s site and from there of course, you will be able to locate additional information. Our Sources area in this document also provides links to their pages listing Reptilian and Amphibian humanoids throughout Human history, the Dagon as well as ufologically related links to similar beings. Due to the number of historical overviews that are currently available, this document will focus on their apparent activities involving the Modern Day Contact phenomenon, which the HR Team is defining as beginning 70 years ago. [1-2]

 

 




It is prudent to bear in mind that not everyone believes the Anunnaki had or have Reptilian traits or that the Reptilians are even related to the Anunnaki. Physicist Michio Kaku does not believe the Anunnaki or any other Advanced Intelligence for that matter, can be classified into a category or civilization type such as Type One, Type Two or Type Three, stating,


“The immensity of the Universe, the Galactic Mind works differently and we’ll talk about the ‘Galactic Mind,’ the ‘Universal Mind,’ and the ‘Supersymmetric Mind’… [These classifications] do not apply to the Anunnaki and other highly developed galactic civilizations simply because the human mind simply cannot understand the extremely high and extraordinary standards / levels of technology and science of civilizations which are millions, perhaps billions of years ahead of us.” [3]



On the other hand, and from the same source, the prolific author Maximillien De Lafayette goes much further outlining an entire future agenda concerning these beings, and it is frightful indeed. Some of the areas he concentrates on, besides providing the year of their arrival, are the Anunnaki’s “spaceships and stargates appearing over major cities,” “clashes with Humans,” and the “Anunnaki’s classification of Humanity into three groups, regarding their level of ‘contamination.’” [4]

 

 

Reptilians and Dracos (or Draconians) may be two different types of beings, or they could be the same species with the same amount of variation among them that we have seen with the Greys and even among Humans, i.e. physical characteristics of height, skin colour and texture. As with many other Extraterrestrials, their activities and physical characteristics overlap quite frequently and they are ofttimes seen together.


Generally speaking, the Dracos are taller than the Reptilians and are sometimes seen with wings that appear atrophied and are not used for actual flight. Both types of beings wear capes with hoods at times to conceal their appearance and, generally speaking, they are described as the most alien appearing beings Abductee-Experiencers report.


The height of Reptilians range from approximately 6 to 8 feet, and their two hands consist of four-fingered claws with webbing and ofttimes external ribbing is visible on their chests and backs. They stand upright and have smooth to rough appearing lizard-like scales instead of skin. Their skin colour is greenish to brownish. Dracos are reported as tall 10 -12 feet in height and their skin appears as gray and mottled. Abductee-Experiencer Ken Bakeman has seen several Dracos with white skin, which he refers to as albino Dracos. There have been approximately three reports of Reptilians with blue skin of which the HR Team is aware and one of the more well known of these is that of Erica Goetsch, who reports positive encounters with them. While the blue skin might seem rare or an aberration to some, the HR Team believe these beings’ skin probably has chameleon characteristics and they are able to adapt to their surroundings and circumstances. Their emotions may also be expressed by the changing colour of their skin. [5]



Reptilians’ faces are described as a cross between humanoid and snake or lizard like. Their heads have a central ridge coming down from the top to a snout like nose which makes their cranial-to-facial appearance casques like. Their eyes are described as being cat like with vertical elongated gold irises and black pupils. Sometimes their irises, and ofttimes the entire eye has small red splotches similar to what in Humans would be termed small subconjunctival haemorrhages. This red blotchiness makes their eyes appear all the more intense.



Some Abductee-Experiences report Reptilians as extremely calm, caring and loving, but they are also often characterized as repulsive and grotesque, with their behaviour being described sometimes as “disgusting.” Their mannerisms are intrusive and forceful. Female Experiencers have reported both rape and pleasurable sex by male Reptilians, but it is difficult to understand how this is physiologically possible. The male sexual organ of the Reptilian is tucked into the body and covered with a flap of skin, and when their reproductive organ is described, it is much too large to be “compatible” with a human female. In other words, one would think it would be physiologically very difficult, if not impossible for sexual intercourse or rape to occur in this manner. [6]


This in no way implies certain subjects did not, in some manner, experience the act; it is a mystery however as to how this is physiologically possible without leaving a Human female physically damaged and requiring surgical repair. It is crucial to remember, as with many telepathic beings, there is the telepathic ability, more than likely enhanced using a neural implant, or via the optic nerve pathways to induce the Abductee-Experiencer to achieve orgasm by stimulating certain brain pathways, and this would explain the sensation(s) they report having experienced.


Explaining why some Abductee-Experiencers come away from these encounters with large claw marks on their bodies, including their inner thighs, as well as vaginal soreness could be explained by a similar action perpetrated upon them but by different means. Rape does not solely involve a biological body part in order to be “rape.” In the end, the rape act or sexual act that occurs to the Experiencer feels very real and therefore is very real and should not be discounted or belittled. There is often great trauma reported after encounters with Reptilian beings by Abductee-Experiencers, especially women. [7]


Reptilian Hybridization


Our research persuades the HR Team that Reptilian appearing beings are most likely as involved and have an interest in the DNA of Humans as much as other beings who are coming to Earth. Some theorize the Reptilian line has always been on Earth and they are its original inhabitants, or at least have been here much longer than Humans. If they are in fact the Anunnaki, they have apparently staked their claim to humanity and to the Earth in various ancient texts, most notably the Babylonian Creation Mythos. [8]

From our research, they appear to be the best of the best when it comes to subterfuge utilizing camouflage techniques. It is not only the Blond Nordics who can transform into Reptilians, but Reptilians have also reportedly transformed into Blond Nordics. We are in fact determining that some of the Blond Nordic reports are of Reptilian influence.

We must again ask: Why do they do this and what is their true physiological form? Are Reptilians really Reptilians and are Blond Nordics really as they appear? Could both species be something completely different from the image they choose Abductee-Experiencers to see them? Additionally, it has been theorized that Reptilians mated with ancient Blond Nordics and what we are encountering today as Blond Nordics are actually Hybrids of the Reptilians and original Blond Nordics. According to David Icke, the Anunnaki, who are deities of Sumerian, Assyrian, Akkadian and Babylonian culture, “bred with the Blond Nordics to produce a race of Human slave masters” [some might say monsters] “called the Aryans.” Icke claims they also retain many Reptilian traits, not so much physical traits, but ideological ones. Human DNA – Again

As mentioned previously, Reptilians appear to be nearly as involved in using Human DNA in the creation of their own particular version of Human Hybrids as the Greys and those beings who act as their helpers, aides, assistants, biologically created drones, or whatever other designations people have given them. As a review, these include the Greys of Type I through Types IV, as well as the Synthoids; Brown Dwarfs and various stages of Hybrids. [10]


An example of the Reptilian-Dracos’ interest in Human DNA was clearly indicated in a 2006 case involving Reptilians when an Abductee-Experiencer observed a Human woman who had just given birth:


“She appeared to be in her late twenties and had a fair complexion and long blond hair. She had on a light blue hospital top and a sheet was covering her up to her waist. She looked very depressed or was in a trance as she was staring off and looking downward to her left and away from the doctors, nurses and her baby.”


The witness stated the doctors and nurses seemed pleased about the birth of this woman’s baby and that salt or brine water was required for its survival. Interestingly, the baby was inside of a whitish, semi opaque, bubble-like membrane that was approximately 18 inches in diameter, and the infant was visible within this apparent “external womb.” It was placed next to the woman with the umbilical cord or some kind of tube still attached. There was also fluid inside of the womb containing the foetus or baby.


One of the doctors told the Abductee-Experiencer about this woman’s baby, stating to her telepathically and somewhat commandingly and in a deep “voice:”  “The child is a boy and the man of the child is a Dargon” [11 - Dagon]


It is apparent this foetus or baby was part Human and part something else from the description of the Human mother and the artificial womb that lay next to her. Could this baby have been a Hybrid and in fact part Dagon? Historically, the beings called the Dagon were described as being very different from Humans of today in that they were “half humanoid and half fish.” Another name for these beings is The Nommo, an alleged species of amphibious beings who are said to have come to Earth from the Sirius star system and were responsible for the creation of Humans through genetic engineering. If you have read all of the articles in this section in the order they have been presented, you may rightfully ask: How many times do we need to hear the same story of beings coming from other planets and manipulating the DNA of Humans and creating offspring before we admit these seemingly obvious events are irrefutably tied together with our own Human Mythos? Perhaps the ancient histories that “scientific” and “sceptical” people refer to as Myths are not so mythical after all; if they are, then our present day Human existence might be deemed the same.
